About Me:

Student out of Birmingham, AL

Hi, I'm Ryan Grayson. I live in Birmingham, AL with my partner, Missy, my two dogs, Molly and Max, and my cat, Meenoo. My time is spent mostly with the above mentioned as well as with friends, shooting photos and working on and riding Harley-Davidson motorcycles.

I've spent most of my working life in the service industry. I've been a cashier, a line cook, a prep cook, a dishwasher, a waiter's assistant, a bar back. My most recent form was a bartender and I succeeded in that position for the past 7 years. As I moved into my 30's I started to entertain moving out from behind the bar. The money was inconsistant, I no longer desired the lifestyle and as I peered into the future I was unhappy to see myself still bartending. So in late 2019 I decided to make a move; I took a job as a Brewer's Assistant at Good People Brewing Company. However, shortly after I took this position I was laid off due to the COVID-19 pandemic. While this unforseen circumstance created some definite struggles it also forced a positive pivot in my life. After months of not knowing what I was going to do I finally decided to take action. That's when I enrolled in the TrueCoders 9 week coding bootcamp. I've enjoyed the challenges of learning coding languages and techniques and I look forward to progressing in the development field.
